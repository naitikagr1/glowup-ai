# GlowUp AI 🚀

GlowUp AI is an AI-powered personal growth platform designed to help people understand where they are today, identify what is holding them back, and create a personalized roadmap toward becoming the best version of themselves.

## Problem

Most people know they want to improve their lives, health, confidence, discipline, career, and mindset, but they don't know where to start.

Advice online is often generic, overwhelming, and not personalized.

## Solution

GlowUp AI analyzes a user's current habits, goals, strengths, and weaknesses to generate a customized self-improvement roadmap.

Instead of generic motivation, users receive actionable steps tailored to their personal growth journey.
